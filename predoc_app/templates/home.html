Predoc - Preconsultation report assistant

- Predoc helps patients organize their medical details, symptoms, and lifestyle data into structured preconsultation reports.

- So doctors can understand your health story faster, and focus more on accurate diagnosis than data collection.

The Problem

Visiting a doctor without proper preparation often leads to rushed, incomplete consultations. Patients forget key symptoms, struggle to recall medical history, or bring scattered reports from multiple labs and clinics.

Doctors, in turn, spend the first several minutes of each consultation trying to reconstruct the patient’s background — leaving less time for focused diagnosis or discussion.

This results in repeated tests, incomplete advice, and patient frustration — not because doctors aren’t skilled, but because the information isn’t presented clearly at the right time.

The Predoc Solution

Predoc bridges this gap by acting as your preconsultation report assistant. It guides you step-by-step to record symptoms, medical history, lifestyle details, and ongoing medications — automatically structuring them into a clear, doctor-ready health summary.

Predoc doesn’t replace the doctor — it simply helps both sides begin the conversation better prepared.

Key Features of Predoc
